What is Quick Fix and how does it work?

Quick Fix is a self-correcting test that will give you possible explanations and remedies based on symptoms you have. Quick Fix will first ask you which region of the head you are feeling symptoms in (head/face, mouth, ears, nose, or eyes). Then, the test will ask you a series of questions relating to your symptoms. Based on your answers to the questions, Quick Fix will attempt to give you explanations and "quick fixes" for your symptoms. However, in cases when the condition seems serious, Quick Fix may advise you to contact a medical professional.

What does it cover?

This test covers illnesses of the head or conditions where symptoms are seem in the head area.

What does this test do?

This test offers insights into possible causes of symptoms that you are feeling. The information you enter is completely anonymous. Please note that this test should not be used in emergencies is not a qualified diagnosis from a trained medical professional.
