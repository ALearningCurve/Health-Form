Welcome to ASAP Analysis!

Do you need help understanding symptoms felt in your head?

What is ASAP Analysis and how does it work?

ASAP Analysis is a self-correcting online examination that will give possible explanations for symptoms felt in the head. The test will ask you a series of questions relating to your symptoms and attempt to give you explanations based on the answers you give. ASAP Analysis will give you a list of possible conditions that you may have ranked in order by their likelihood.

When should I use this test?

This test can be used whenever symptoms arise that are present in the head. However, please note that this test should not be used in emergency situations and is not an official diagnosis from a medical professional.

Is my information safe and anonymous?

All the information you enter is completely anonymous, is not associated with your identity, and will not be shared in any way.
